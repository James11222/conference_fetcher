# Conference preferences

I'm interested in the following topics
- Cosmology
- Cosmological Simulations (both periodic big box and zoom-in simulations)
- Galaxy Formation
- Cross correlation synergy between surveys (spectroscopic and photometric)
- Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) and it's secondaries
- EUCLID, LSST, DESI, HSC, DES, KiDS
- weak lensing
- dark matter halos
- the cosmic web
- massive neutrinos
- higher order statistics
- effective field theory of large scale structure
- Supermassive Black Hole (SMBH) growth and evolution
- Cosmic Dawn
- epoch of reionization
- Machine learning in the context of astronomy and cosmology
- Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN) and their feedback
- the circumgalactic medium
- fast radio bursts
- kSZ effect, tSZ effect, X-rays
- baryonic feedback

I am not interested in conferences on the following topics 
- exoplanets
- stellar physics
- supernovae
- compact objects (neutron stars and white dwarfs)
- galactic archeology
- plasma physics
- star formation
- solar system dynamics
- interferometry
- solar wind
- gravitational waves
- instrument design
- the local universe

Generally try to avoid showing me conferences that have an abstract submission deadline that has already passed, if there is no date yet available for the abstract submission deadline this is fine. 

If you are on the fence about including a conference, include it. I'd rather see it than miss a potential conference of interest.
